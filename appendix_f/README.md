## Appendix F — Top Acts and Layer Rank Tables

This appendix supports the Full-Network Top Acts and Cross-Layer Rank Flow sections by reporting the data behind Figures 7.10 and 7.11. The full-network top-50 table and the layer-rank tables are generated from the current centrality analysis outputs. Figures 7.9, 7.10, and 7.11 are static Matplotlib 3.9.4 renders of the same analysis outputs. Katz scores in Table F.1 are rounded to six decimal places; the top-50 range is 0.056790 to 0.285505.

The layer-rank tables list every Act that appears in at least one top-50 list across the eight expansion layers or the full network (59 unique Acts). A dash means that the Act is not in that stage's top 50. Table F.4 then summarises the 51 Acts that appear in two or more stages; positive rank change means movement toward rank 1 between the first and last listed stages.
